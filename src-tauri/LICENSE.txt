PRIVACY POLICY

gbc.chat Desktop is a native desktop client that loads the gbc.chat web application.

Information We Collect:
- Account information: email address, username, and display name
- Profile data: avatar, bio, and status (if you choose to set them)
- Messages: text, images, and files you send through the platform
- Usage data: login timestamps, online status, and server membership

How We Use Your Information:
- To provide and maintain the chat service
- To display your profile and messages to other users in servers you join
- To send notifications you have opted into (mentions, DMs)
- To enforce our terms of service and prevent abuse

Data Sharing:
We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Desktop Application:
The desktop wrapper itself does NOT collect, store, or transmit any additional data beyond what the web application does. It communicates exclusively with gbc.chat servers.

No third-party tracking cookies or analytics are used.

Full privacy policy: https://gbc.chat/privacy
